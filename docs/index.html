Why it exists

Most budgeting apps are noisy. Au Jour Le Jour is the opposite: a clean ledger that keeps essentials tight and makes the monthly survival number obvious. It is designed for discipline, not lifestyle creep.

  • Single-source-of-truth ledger you own
  • Instant clarity on what is required vs. optional
  • Motivating but simple: no charts, just the numbers that matter

Local-first by default

The app runs entirely on your machine. Data lives in one SQLite file and survives reloads, reboots, and travel. Export any month as CSV or backup the full ledger as JSON.

Install locally

Runs on macOS, Windows, and Linux. No cloud. No accounts. Once installed, open the app on your local network. 

git clone https://github.com/Fuzzyslippers412/AuJourLeJour.git
cd AuJourLeJour
./start.sh
# open http://localhost:6709

One command starts everything. The SQLite ledger lives in data/.

What you get

  • Monthly essentials dashboard
  • Recurring bill templates
  • Payment log with undo
  • Backup/export built-in

Integration-ready

Au Jour Le Jour is designed to plug into the MyCasaPro Finance Agent (Mamdou) via a stable local contract. The ledger remains authoritative; external agents can only read summaries and propose explicit actions.
